1975

🛰️ Aryabhata

First Indian Satellite

India's first satellite, named after ancient mathematician. Launched by Soviet Union.

Mission Details:

  • Launch Date: 19 April 1975
  • Launch Site: Kapustin Yar, Russia
  • Weight: 360 kg
  • Objective: X-ray astronomy, aeronomics, solar physics
  • Status: Deactivated after 5 days
1980

🚀 SLV-3

First Indian Launch Vehicle

India's first satellite launch vehicle. Successfully placed Rohini satellite in orbit.

Mission Details:

  • Launch Date: 18 July 1980
  • Launch Site: Sriharikota, India
  • Significance: India became 6th nation with orbital launch capability
  • Payload: Rohini RS-1 satellite
  • Status: Successful
1982

📡 INSAT-1A

First Communication Satellite

India's first multipurpose communication and meteorology satellite.

Mission Details:

  • Launch Date: 10 April 1982
  • Purpose: Telecommunications, TV broadcast, meteorology
  • Weight: 1152 kg
  • Status: Failed after 6 months due to power issues
2008

🌙 Chandrayaan-1

First Lunar Mission

India's first mission to Moon. Discovered water molecules on lunar surface!

Mission Details:

  • Launch Date: 22 October 2008
  • Launch Vehicle: PSLV-C11
  • Major Discovery: Water molecules on Moon
  • Orbits: 3400+ around Moon
  • Status: Mission completed successfully
2013

🔴 Mangalyaan (Mars Orbiter Mission)

First Interplanetary Mission

India became first Asian nation and first in world to reach Mars in maiden attempt!

Mission Details:

  • Launch Date: 5 November 2013
  • Mars Orbit: 24 September 2014
  • Cost: ₹450 crore (cheaper than Hollywood movie Gravity!)
  • Achievement: First country to succeed in first attempt
  • Status: Successfully operated till 2022
2019

🌙 Chandrayaan-2

Moon Landing Attempt

Attempted soft landing on Moon's south pole. Orbiter still functioning perfectly.

Mission Details:

  • Launch Date: 22 July 2019
  • Components: Orbiter, Lander (Vikram), Rover (Pragyan)
  • Landing Attempt: 7 September 2019
  • Status: Lander lost contact, but Orbiter still working
  • Achievement: Valuable data from lunar orbit
2023

🌙 Chandrayaan-3

Successful Moon Landing

Historic! India became 4th country to land on Moon and 1st on south pole!

Mission Details:

  • Launch Date: 14 July 2023
  • Landing Date: 23 August 2023
  • Landing Site: Moon's South Pole
  • Achievement: Soft landing + Rover operations successful
  • Status: Mission accomplished! 🎉
